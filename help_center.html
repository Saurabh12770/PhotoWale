Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on "Login" at the top right, then select "Sign Up". You can register as a client or a photographer. Fill in your details and verify your email to get started.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, Paytm, Google Pay, and more. All transactions are secure.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes, you can cancel from your dashboard. Refund amounts depend on how close to the event you cancel. See our refund policy for details.

How do I contact support?

You can reach us via email, phone, or live chat below. Our team is available Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM IST.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption and are PCI-DSS compliant. Your payment details are never stored on our servers.

How do I become a photographer?

Click on "Become Pro" and fill out the application. Our team will review your portfolio and get back to you within 3-5 days.

What is the PhotoWale Pro subscription?

Pro is a paid subscription for photographers that reduces commission to 10%, gives priority ranking, and includes advanced analytics.

Can I use a gift card for any service?

Yes, gift cards can be used for any photography service on the platform.
