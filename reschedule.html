What You Can Reschedule

  • Date & Time: Subject to photographer's availability.
  • Location: Within same city (photographer must agree).
  • Package: Upgrade/downgrade – price difference applies.
  • Add-ons: Extra hours, second shooter, albums, etc.
  • Photographer: Only in emergency (we'll help find substitute).
  • Event type: Major changes (e.g., wedding to corporate) may require new booking.

Photographers understand that plans change. They are notified immediately when you request a reschedule. They can accept or counter-offer alternative dates. If they are unavailable, we help find a replacement. Photographers receive the reschedule fee (if any) as compensation for lost time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I reschedule multiple times?

Yes, but fees may apply after the first free reschedule. Each change must be requested at least 48 hours before the original event time.

What if the photographer is unavailable on the new date?

You can choose another date from their calendar or we can help find an alternative photographer of similar quality.

Will the price change if I reschedule?

The package price remains the same. However, if you upgrade/downgrade, the difference will be charged or refunded accordingly.

What about rescheduling due to weather?

Outdoor shoots affected by bad weather can be rescheduled free of charge if notified at least 12 hours in advance. Last-minute weather changes will be handled case-by-case.

Can I reschedule a booking that was already rescheduled once?

Yes, but standard fees apply from the second reschedule onward. Please check the fee table above.

How do I know if my reschedule request is accepted?

You'll receive an email and notification in your account once the photographer confirms. Usually within 24 hours.

Is there a limit on how many times I can reschedule?

No limit, but fees may apply after the first change. Photographer must agree each time.

What if I need to reschedule within 48 hours due to emergency?

Contact support immediately. With valid proof (medical certificate, etc.), we may allow a free reschedule.

Can I reschedule to a date more than 6 months later?

Yes, as long as the photographer is available. Your payment will remain valid.

Do I need to pay again if the new package is more expensive?

Yes, you'll pay the difference. If cheaper, we'll refund the difference.

What if the photographer reschedules on their end?

We'll notify you immediately. You can accept the new date or get a full refund.

Can I reschedule a gift card booking?

Yes, same rules apply. The gift card remains valid.

How long does it take to process a reschedule?

Once photographer confirms, it's instant. Photographers usually respond within 24 hours.

What if I reschedule and then cancel later?

Cancellation fees are based on the original event date, not the rescheduled one.

Is there a fee for rescheduling a Pro membership?

No, Pro subscriptions are not affected by booking reschedules.
