Our Story

PhotoWale was born in 2023 out of a simple observation: booking a professional photographer was unnecessarily complicated. Clients struggled to find trusted professionals, while talented photographers faced inconsistent work and payment hassles. We set out to build a platform that brings transparency, trust, and technology to the photography industry. Today, we're proud to be the go-to choice for thousands of customers and photographers across India.
