Delivery Process – Step by Step

1. Shoot Day

Photographer captures your event. You may preview some shots on the spot (optional).

2. Culling & Editing

Photographer selects best images, performs color correction, retouching, etc. (2-5 days).

3. Gallery Creation

Images are uploaded to a private, password-protected online gallery. (1 day).

4. Notification

You receive an email with the gallery link and download instructions.

5. Download & Share

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Typically 5-10 business days after the shoot. Express editing available (2-3 days) for an extra fee.

Can I get physical prints?

Yes, many photographers offer prints, albums, and USB drives. Check package details or request a quote.

What if I don't like the photos?

Request revisions through our Resolution Center. Photographers must accommodate reasonable changes. If unresolved, we mediate.

Are the photos watermarked?

Final images are watermark-free. Previews may have watermarks.

Can I download directly?

Yes, the online gallery allows one-click download of all images in high resolution.

How are photographers verified?

ID, portfolio review, background check, and client references. See selection criteria above.

What if my photos are lost?

Photographers are required to keep backups for 30 days. If files are lost, we ensure they re-upload.

Can I order extra prints later?

Yes, you can contact the photographer directly to order additional prints or albums.

Is there a guarantee on delivery time?

Photographers commit to a timeline in their package. If delayed, you may be eligible for partial refund.

What file formats are delivered?

Usually JPEG (high quality). Some photographers offer TIFF/RAW upon request (may incur fee).

Can I share the gallery with family?

Yes, you can share the gallery link (password may be required). They can view but not download unless you allow.

How long are galleries available?

Galleries are active for 30 days after delivery. After that, a retrieval fee may apply.

What if the photographer's style doesn't match?

We recommend reviewing portfolios carefully. If you're unhappy, contact us within 7 days.

Do you offer RAW files?

Most photographers do not, as RAW requires processing. Some may for an additional fee.

Can I request specific edits?

Yes, communicate with your photographer before the shoot. They'll try to accommodate.
