General Questions

What is PhotoWale?

PhotoWale is a marketplace that connects clients with verified professional photographers. Clients can browse portfolios, compare packages, and book instantly. Photographers get powerful tools to manage their business and grow their client base.

How do I create an account?

Click on "Login" at the top right and select "Sign Up". You can register as a client or a photographer. Fill in your details and verify your email to get started.

Is PhotoWale available in my city?

We are currently operational in Ahmedabad, Surat, Mumbai, and Pune, with plans to expand to Delhi NCR and Bengaluru soon. You can check the search box on the home page to see available photographers in your area.

Is PhotoWale free to use?

For clients, browsing and booking is free. Photographers pay a commission on bookings (15% for free accounts, 10% for Pro members).

For Clients

How do I book a photographer?

Simply use the search bar on the home page to find photographers based on event type, location, and date. Browse profiles, compare packages, and click "Book Now" to confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email once the photographer accepts.

Can I cancel or reschedule a booking?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule through your account dashboard. Cancellation policies vary by photographer, but most allow free cancellation up to 7 days before the event. Please check the specific photographer's policy before booking.

How are photographers verified?

Every photographer on PhotoWale goes through a verification process that includes ID verification, portfolio review, and background checks. We also display real client reviews and ratings to help you make an informed decision.

What if I'm not satisfied with the photos?

We have a customer satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the delivered work, you can raise a dispute within 7 days. Our team will mediate and ensure a fair resolution, which may include a partial refund or a reshoot.

Can I communicate with the photographer before booking?

Yes, you can send messages to photographers through our platform to ask questions before booking.

For Photographers

How do I join as a photographer?

Click on "Become Pro" in the top bar. Fill out the application form with your portfolio, experience, and services. Our team will review your application and get back to you within 3-5 business days.

What are the commission fees?

We charge a 15% commission on each booking made through our platform. This covers payment processing, customer support, and marketing. We also offer a premium subscription (PhotoWale Pro) that reduces commission to 10% for a monthly fee.

How do I get paid?

Payments are released to your bank account 2 days after the event date, provided the client hasn't raised any dispute. You can track your earnings and withdraw funds via the photographer dashboard.

Can I set my own prices and packages?

Absolutely! You have full control over your pricing, packages, and availability. You can customize your offerings from your dashboard at any time.

How do I get more bookings?

Complete your profile, upload high-quality photos, and consider upgrading to Pro for priority ranking and more visibility.

Payments & Refunds

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and popular wallets like Paytm and Google Pay. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

When will I be charged?

Your payment is collected at the time of booking. The amount is held securely by PhotoWale and released to the photographer after the event, ensuring protection for both parties.

How do refunds work?

Refunds are processed based on the photographer's cancellation policy. If you cancel within the free cancellation period, you'll receive a full refund. For other cases, please contact our support team.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, the price you see includes all taxes and fees. There are no hidden charges.

Can I get an invoice?

Yes, after each booking you can download a GST invoice from your account dashboard.

Technical Support

I'm having trouble logging in. What should I do?

Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link. If the issue persists, contact our support team at support@photowale.com.

The website is loading slowly. What can I do?

Try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. If the problem continues, check your internet connection or reach out to us for assistance.

How do I update my account information?

Log in to your account, go to "My Profile" or "Dashboard", and edit your details. Don't forget to save changes before leaving the page.

Is the app available for mobile?

Yes, you can download the PhotoWale app from Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

How do I delete my account?

Please contact support to request account deletion. We'll process it within 7 days.
