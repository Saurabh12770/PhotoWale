Refund Overview

Full Refund

If cancelled 7+ days before event, or if photographer cancels. Also applicable for duplicate charges and technical errors.

Partial Refund

3-7 days: 75% refund; 48h-3 days: 50% refund; based on photographer's policy.

No Refund

Less than 48 hours notice, no-show, or change of mind within 48h window.

Processing Time

5-7 business days (cards), 1-2 days (UPI/wallets).

Conditions for Refund

  • Cancellation made within the allowed time frame (based on photographer's policy).
  • Photographer failed to show up or delivered no work.
  • Service not provided as described – quality dispute resolved in your favor.
  • Duplicate or incorrect charge (e.g., double billing).
  • Technical issue prevented booking confirmation but payment was taken.
  • Photographer cancels for any reason (full refund + voucher).
  • Event cancelled due to government restrictions (pandemic, etc.) – case-by-case.
  • Client death or medical emergency (documentation required).

Refund Exceptions

  • No-show without prior cancellation (client didn't show up).
  • Change of mind after 48-hour cancellation window.
  • Disputes raised after 7 days from event date.
  • Partial delivery issues where photographer agrees to re-edit (no refund, but rework).
  • Force majeure events (natural disasters) – handled case-by-case, may be rescheduled instead of refund.
  • Client requests changes after work has begun (cancellation fees apply).
  • Gift card purchases – non-refundable.
  • Pro subscription fees – no refund for partial month, but can cancel for future.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do refunds take?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after approval. Credit card refunds may take an additional 2-3 days depending on your bank. UPI/wallet refunds are usually instant.

Can I get a refund if I'm unhappy with the photos?

If the photos do not meet the agreed-upon quality, you can raise a dispute within 7 days. Our team will review and may issue a partial or full refund based on the severity.

What if the photographer cancels?

You will receive a 100% refund plus a 10% voucher for your next booking. We'll also help you find an alternative photographer if needed.

Do I get a refund if I reschedule?

Rescheduling is not a refund; your payment is transferred to the new date. However, if you downgrade your package, the price difference will be refunded. If you upgrade, you pay the difference.

Can I get a refund for a gift card?

Gift cards are non-refundable but can be used for any booking. Unused gift cards do not expire. However, if a gift card was purchased fraudulently, please contact support.

What is the dispute process?

You can raise a dispute from your booking page within 7 days of the event. Provide evidence (photos, messages). Our team will review and contact both parties within 3 business days. A decision is made based on the evidence.

Will I be charged a cancellation fee?

Yes, if you cancel within 7 days of the event, a cancellation fee applies (25% for 3-7 days, 50% for 48h-3 days). This covers the photographer's time and lost opportunity.

Can I get a refund for a Pro subscription?

Pro subscriptions are billed monthly/yearly. You can cancel anytime, but refunds are not provided for partial months. The benefits continue until the end of the paid period.

What if I paid via EMI?

Refunds for EMI transactions are processed to your card, and the loan amount is reversed. This may take 7-10 business days. Interest paid (if any) is not refunded by PhotoWale.

Do I need to return any physical products for a refund?

If your package included physical items (prints, albums), you may need to return them at your cost. Refunds for such items are processed after receipt.

What if I was charged the wrong amount?

Contact support immediately with proof of correct amount. We'll refund the excess within 2 business days.

How do I check the status of my refund?

You can track refund status in your account under "My Bookings" → "Refund Status". You'll also receive email updates.

Need Help with a Refund?

Our support team is here to assist you with any refund-related queries.
