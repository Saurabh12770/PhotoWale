Press FAQ

When was PhotoWale founded?

PhotoWale was founded in 2023 by Saurabh Kumar and team to simplify booking professional photographers.

How many photographers are on the platform?

As of March 2026, we have over 500 verified photographers across major Indian cities.

What cities do you operate in?

Currently Ahmedabad, Surat, Mumbai, Pune, Delhi NCR, and Bengaluru. Expanding to Hyderabad and Chennai soon.

Can I interview a company spokesperson?

Yes. Please email press@photowale.com with your request and we'll coordinate.

How many bookings have been made?

Over 8,000 successful bookings as of February 2026.

Who are your investors?

We are backed by Venture Partners, AngelList syndicate, and several angel investors from the photography industry.

Do you have any brand partnerships?

Yes, we partner with Canon, Nikon, Sony, and Razorpay.

Where can I find your logo and branding assets?

Download our press kit above for logos, guidelines, and more.
