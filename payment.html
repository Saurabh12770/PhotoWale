🎯 Milestone‑based conditional payments

Your money is held securely and released to the photographer step by step, only when both sides are happy. This brings total transparency – just like an escrow but smarter.

🔹 Booking (10%) 📸 Shoot day (60%) 📦 Delivery (80%) ⭐ Review (100%)

At booking stage: 10% paid to photographer (deposit).

1. Booking (10%)

Non‑refundable deposit to confirm the date. Released immediately after photographer accepts.

2. Shoot day (↗️60%)

Additional 50% (total 60%) released 48h after shoot, if client doesn't dispute that the event happened.

3. Delivery (↗️80%)

Additional 20% (total 80%) released upon delivery of final gallery / photos.

4. Client review (↗️100%)

Final 20% released based on review & satisfaction – admin mediation if needed.

How the final 20% (review milestone) works

✅ If client gives positive review (⭐ 4 or 5)

  • Final 20% is automatically released within 24h
  • Photographer gets full payout
  • Client may also get loyalty coins

⚠️ If client gives negative review or raises issue

  • Final 20% is frozen, admin team starts review
  • Admin examines work quality, communication, contract
  • Possible outcomes: release full 20%, partial refund from it, or in severe cases refund from earlier milestones

Why this protects both sides

Client benefits

You don't pay fully until you're satisfied. If work is poor, you get money back. No more “paid in full and photographer vanished”.

Photographer benefits

You receive partial payments throughout, reducing risk of non‑payment. Fair admin review prevents false complaints.

Milestone release conditions – detailed

What triggers the 60% (shoot day) release?

After the shoot, we send a confirmation link to the client. If they click “Everything OK” within 48 hours, the money is released. If they do nothing, it releases automatically after 7 days (unless dispute raised).

What if the photographer doesn't deliver?

If delivery is delayed beyond the agreed date, the client can raise a dispute. The final 20% stays frozen, and we may release a partial refund or push the photographer to deliver.

Can admin touch money already released?

Once money is released to the photographer, it cannot be pulled back. However, in extreme fraud cases, we have insurance to compensate clients. Our milestone system minimises such needs.

How long does admin review take?

Typically 2‑3 business days. Both parties can submit evidence. Our experts (ex‑photographers) evaluate quality and adherence to contract.

What if client never leaves a review?

After 14 days from delivery, if no review, we consider it as implicit satisfaction and release the final 20% automatically.
