How Escrow Works – Detailed

When a client books a photographer, the full amount is charged immediately but held in a separate escrow account. This money is not accessible to the photographer until after the event. After the event, the client has 48 hours to inspect the delivered work and raise any issues. If no dispute is raised, the funds are released to the photographer within 2 business days. This system ensures that photographers are paid for their work and clients receive what they paid for.

Dispute Process – Detailed

When a dispute is raised, both parties are notified and asked to provide evidence (photos, messages, contracts). Our team reviews within 3 business days. We may request additional information. Based on the evidence, we decide on a fair resolution: full/partial refund, credit, or no refund. Both parties accept the decision as final.

Payment Policies & FAQ

When is my card charged?

Your payment is authorized at booking, but captured only after photographer accepts. If declined, the hold is released.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are automatic based on cancellation time. Request via Resolution Center within 7 days.

Are there hidden charges?

No. The price includes all taxes and fees.

Can I pay partially?

Some photographers offer installments. Check package details.

What is PhotoWale guarantee?

We guarantee service as booked or your money back.

How do I update payment method?

You can't change after booking. For future bookings, update in your profile.

Is my payment info saved?

We don't store full card numbers. Tokenized for future use with your consent.
