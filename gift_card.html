Frequently Asked Questions

How do I redeem a gift card?

During checkout, enter the gift card code in the "Gift Card / Voucher" field. The amount will be deducted from your total.

Can I use a gift card for any photographer?

Yes, gift cards are valid for all photographers and services on the PhotoWale platform.

Do gift cards expire?

Yes, gift cards are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. Expired cards cannot be used.

Can I get a refund on a gift card?

Gift cards are non-refundable. However, you can transfer them to another person.

Can I schedule delivery for a future date?

Yes, you can choose a future delivery date during checkout. The gift card will be sent via email on that date.

Is there a fee for gift cards?

No, we don't charge any additional fees for gift cards. You pay only the face value.
